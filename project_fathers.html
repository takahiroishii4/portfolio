Father's Playbook

Redesign of a mobile app to encourage fathers to engage in the prenatal process

My Main Role UX designer
Members Paul Toprac - Manager, Kyle Sotengco - Android engineer, Yongye Zhu - iOS engineer

Redesign Concept

Father's Playbook is designed to encourage and educate fathers to engage in the prenatal process. It provides articles, a budget calculation function, and progress visualization to make them stay motivated to prepare for delivery. Here are some designs, but we are still working on the design through iteration process of user research, usability testing, and prototyping. Please come back here to see the updates.
